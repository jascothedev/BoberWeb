ABOUT

Bober

Welcome to Bober Coffee & Street Food, your go-to spot for high-quality street food and coffee, just steps away from the sandy beaches of Montenegro's beautiful city, Bar.
Bober offers a delicious fusion of street food favorites served in a vibrant and welcoming atmosphere.
Indulge in our mouthwatering burgers, gourmet hotdogs, and flavorful tacos, all crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure top-notch quality with every bite.
And don't forget to pair your meal with our premium selection of freshly brewed coffee, expertly prepared to perfection. Our baristas are dedicated to satisfying your caffeine cravings.
Whether you're on the go or looking to relax after a day at the beach, Bober provides a convenient and inviting space to enjoy, delicious coffee and food also don't forget our friendly service :)
Join us at Bober, where coffee and street food meets quality, and every dish is served with a seaside charm.
